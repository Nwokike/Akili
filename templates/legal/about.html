About Akili

Our Mission

Akili is Nigeria's first personal AI-powered study platform designed to make one-on-one tutoring accessible through dynamic, responsive learning. We believe every student deserves access to quality education, regardless of their location or financial situation.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional past questions platforms, Akili generates all content on demand using advanced AI technology. This means:

  • No copyright issues or outdated content
  • Personalized learning paths tailored to your needs
  • Instant explanations for any topic, anytime
  • Syllabus-grounded mock exams that mirror real exam standards
  • Progressive Web App that works offline

Supported Class Levels

We support the complete Nigerian secondary education curriculum:

  • JS1 - JS3 - Junior Secondary School (Years 1-3)
  • SS1 - SS3 - Senior Secondary School (Years 1-3)

Freemium Model

We operate on a freemium model to keep education accessible:

  • 10 free Tutor Credits daily (resets automatically)
  • No credit card required to sign up
  • Earn extra credits by referring friends (+2 per referral, max 30)
  • Purchase additional credits for intensive study sessions
