About Akili

Nigeria's first AI-powered personal exam prep platform

Our Mission

At Akili, we believe every Nigerian student deserves access to quality one-on-one tutoring. Traditional tutoring is expensive and often inaccessible. That's why we built an AI-powered platform that makes personalized learning available to everyone.

What Makes Us Different

AI-Powered Learning: Unlike traditional past questions apps, Akili generates all content on-demand using advanced AI. This means you get fresh, personalized explanations every time.

Syllabus-Grounded: All our content follows official JAMB, WAEC, NECO, and JSS syllabuses. You study exactly what you need for your exams.

Two-Pass Validation: Every lesson is validated by a second AI to ensure accuracy before you see it. Report errors and we regenerate the content.

Freemium Model: Start with 10 free credits daily. No credit card required. Pay only when you need more, or refer friends to unlock up to 30 daily credits.

How It Works

  1. Create Your Course: Select your exam type (JAMB, WAEC, NECO, JSS) and subject
  2. AI Generates Modules: Get 15 personalized modules based on the official syllabus
  3. Study & Practice: Read lessons, take quizzes, and ask your AI tutor questions
  4. Take Mock Exams: Generate full practice exams to test your knowledge
  5. Track Progress: See your improvement and identify weak areas

Our Technology

Akili uses a 4-tier AI Smart Fallback system to ensure reliability:

  • Tier 1: Gemini 2.5 Flash (Free) - Primary AI
  • Tier 2: Groq API (Free) - Backup AI
  • Tier 3: Gemini Paid - Secondary backup
  • Tier 4: Circuit Breaker - Graceful fallback message

Built for Nigerian Students

We understand the challenges Nigerian students face:

  • Limited access to quality tutoring
  • Expensive past question books
  • Need for offline study options
  • Mobile-first learning experience

That's why Akili is a Progressive Web App that works on any device and can be installed for offline access.
