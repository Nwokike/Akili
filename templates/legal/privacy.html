Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 10, 2025

Introduction

Akili ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered study platform.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and communication
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored for authentication
  • Learning Data: Your course progress, quiz attempts, and exam scores
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform to improve our services
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Paystack (we don't store card details)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI tutoring services
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Process payments and manage your credit balance
  • Send important updates about your account
  • Improve our platform and develop new features
  • Manage your referral rewards

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure database storage with regular backups
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Paystack: For secure payment processing
  • Google Gemini & Groq: For AI content generation (anonymized prompts)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your learning data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at:

Email: privacy@akili.ng
