Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use Akili, we collect:

  • Email address and password (for authentication)
  • Usage data (lessons accessed, quizzes taken, exam attempts)
  • Credit usage and purchase history
  • Referral information when you invite friends

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI tutoring service
  • Track your learning progress and credit usage
  • Send important updates about your account
  • Process payments securely through Paystack
  • Award referral bonuses when friends join

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text. Payment information is handled securely by Paystack.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AI Providers: Google Gemini and Groq for AI-powered tutoring
  • Payment Processing: Paystack for secure credit purchases
  • Hosting: Render and Oracle Cloud for platform infrastructure

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Export your learning progress
  • Delete your account at any time

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

7. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact us at: privacy@akili.ng
